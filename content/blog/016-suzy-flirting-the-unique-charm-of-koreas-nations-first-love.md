# Discover the Trend of Suzy-Flirting and Its Impact on Korean Pop Culture

Have you ever heard about “`Suzy-flirting`”?

In Korea, there is a celebrity named Suzy. 

She is also nicknamed “`the nation’s first love`.” 

She started her career in the entertainment industry as an idol and is currently active as both a singer and an actress. She is now loved by many.

### The Viral Smile

Meanwhile, one day in 2023, Suzy appeared on the YouTube channel of her close friend, singer Hyun-ah Jo. Then she introduced her unique and strange smile as a flirting technique.
