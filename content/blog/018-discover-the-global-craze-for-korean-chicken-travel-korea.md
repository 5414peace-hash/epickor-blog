# Korean Chicken is Crazy

Today I would like to talk about Korean food, which surprises people all over the world with its taste. This food? Yes, it’s the `Korean Chicken`.

### Featured on Taste of Home

Recently, Korean chicken was featured on *Taste of Home*, a famous American cooking media. Published in 1993, *Taste of Home* is a credible magazine with an average of 20 million website visitors per month.

### Top-Ranked Fried Chicken

This magazine, published in the United States, the home of chicken, selected the most delicious fried chicken, and Korean chicken took first place, beating all global brands such as KFC and Popeyes. Chicken from a `Korean BBQ brand` won best chicken and best wings.

### Unmatched Flavor

Anyone who has tried Korean chicken will probably be unable to refute this. As the world's love for Korean chicken continues to grow, Korean chicken brand stores are opening up all over the world, so be sure to check it out and try it. Once you try Korean chicken, you won't be able to stop.
