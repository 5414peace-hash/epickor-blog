# Discover Rural Korea - Choncance

In Korea, there's a term called '`choncance`.' It comes from combining '`chon`,' a Korean word that means countryside, and '`cance`' from 'vacance' (vacation).

### Peaceful Retreat

It's like when people tired of city life head to the countryside for some peace and quiet.

### Relaxing Surroundings
