### Kim Soo-hyun's Acting Journey

Kim Soo-hyun, a well-established actor known for his remarkable performances, has starred in several hit dramas. His notable works include the youth drama "`Dream High`," the traditional Korean drama "`The Moon Embracing the Sun`," and the fantasy drama "`My Love from the Star`."

### Recent Success

Kim Soo-hyun has once again achieved box office success with his latest drama "`Queen of Tears`."

### Appearance Fee Revelation

As "Queen of Tears" gained popularity, so did the buzz around Kim Soo-hyun's appearance fee. It was revealed that he was initially offered $600,000 per episode, amounting to around $9,600,000 for the 16-episode series, which left many people astonished.

### Fee Adjustment

However, a correction article later stated that Kim Soo-hyun adjusted his fee, taking into account the production costs, and agreed to receive $220,000 per episode.
