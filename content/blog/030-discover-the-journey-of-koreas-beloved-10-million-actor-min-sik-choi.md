# Korea's No.1 Actor: Min-sik Choi

There is a movie that recently exceeded 10 million viewers in Korea. 

It’s called "`Exhuma`." 

Since the total Korean population is just over 50 million, 10 million viewers in Korea means that 1 in 5 people have seen the movie, which is very special.

### Special Title

So, actors who appear in movies with 10 million viewers get the special title of “`10 million actor`.” Min-sik Choi, a middle-aged Korean actor, became the actor with two 10 million-viewed films in his filmography by appearing in this movie "Exhuma."

### Long Career

Min-sik Choi was `born in 1962`, debuted in 1989, and is already an actor in his 35th year. In Korea, there is strong faith in the acting of actor Min-sik Choi, as he received the grand prize at the country's representative film festival.

### Charming Personality

And above all, Min-sik Choi is so cute for his age. If you look at the way he goes around promoting movies, he shows a friendly image that goes beyond his brilliant career in the film industry and great acting skills.

### Trustworthy Actor

Whether it's a movie or a drama, when people in Korea say `Min-sik Choi` is in it, they believe it and watch it.
