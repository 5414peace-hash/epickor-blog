### Love for Singing

**Karaoke Culture**:

Koreans usually love singing very much. There are about `30,000 karaoke rooms` in Korea alone. For reference, there are `50 million Koreans`. Don't they put all their anger, joy, and sorrow in one place and relieve their stress through the medium of 'song'?
