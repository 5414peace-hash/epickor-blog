### 2. The Defensive Wall: Kim Min-jae and Lee Kang-in
The talent pool is deep. **Kim Min-jae**, known as "The Monster," has become one of the best defenders in the world at clubs like Napoli and Bayern Munich. Meanwhile, **Lee Kang-in** at PSG represents the creative future of Korean football, possessing technical skills that rival the best in Europe.

### 3. Why are they so good? The System
The success of Korean football is rooted in a highly disciplined and rigorous youth system. Players are trained with a focus on technical fundamentals and physical endurance from a very young age. This, combined with the Korean mentality of "Tumhon" (fighting spirit), makes them incredibly resilient on the pitch.

### FAQ: Korean Football
**Q: Which is the most popular football club in Korea?**
A: Domestically, clubs like **FC Seoul** and **Ulsan HD** are popular, but the most followed clubs overall are the ones where Korean stars play, like Tottenham Hotspur or PSG.
**Q: Is the K-League worth watching?**
