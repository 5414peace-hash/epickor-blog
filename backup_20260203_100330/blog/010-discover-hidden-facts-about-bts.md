# 9 Things About BTS You Didn't Know

### Blood Type

None of the 7 members have blood type B.

### B-FREE Controversy

Korean underground rapper B-FREE, who insulted BTS before, was recently booked on charges of interfering with the Korean election campaign.

### Early Debut

The official debut date is June 13, 2013. Unofficially, on September 30, 2010, RM's part appeared in singer Lim Jeong-hee's song "JAE."

### High School Performance

RM and Korean rap superstar Changmo shared a stage during their high school days, and there is a video of them rapping on the same stage.

### Jungkook's Donation

In 2023, Jungkook donated $750,000 to Seoul National University Children's Hospital.

### Producer Bang Si-hyuk's Dedication

Before BTS's debut, Producer Bang Si-hyuk owed billions of won. Despite concerns, he debuted the 7 talented members, feeling a sense of duty.
