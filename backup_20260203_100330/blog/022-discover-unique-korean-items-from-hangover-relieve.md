# Korean Things You Might Find Fascinating

In Korea, `hangover relievers` of various flavors and price ranges are easily available at convenience stores or supermarkets. There is water for hangover relief, powder form, jelly form, etc. You can choose according to your preference. 

If you take a hangover reliever before or after drinking, you will feel much better.

### Snow Duck Toy

This is a toy that allows you to create a duck shape with snow. 

Koreans use this toy to create an army of hundreds of ducks in the winter.

### Rolled Colored Pencils

Did you know that Korea is famous for its cute characters and various stationery products? 

Of course, there are colored pencils that can be sharpened in Korea as well. 

Rolled colored pencils that don't stain your hands are the basics, and there are many high-quality and interesting stationery items, such as colored mechanical pencil leads.

### Food Scissors

They look no different from regular scissors, but in Korea, scissors are often used when cooking, so there are food scissors. 

Of course, they never use office scissors and food scissors interchangeably.
